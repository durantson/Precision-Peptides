Redox Research Reagent

NAD+ is a critical coenzyme found in all living cells. In laboratory research, it is investigated for its role as a substrate for Sirtuins (SIRT1-7) and Poly(ADP-ribose) polymerases (PARPs), serving as a central regulator of cellular aging and DNA repair mechanisms.

Solubility Highly Water Soluble
Unit Size 500mg / 1000mg

Biochemical Context

Sirtuin Activation: Research utilizes high-purity NAD+ to study the deacetylation of proteins that regulate stress response and mitochondrial biogenesis. It is a mandatory co-substrate for SIRT1 activity.

Analytical Stability: NAD+ is hygroscopic. Our lyophilized preparation is sealed under argon to prevent premature hydrolysis. Store at -20°C in an airtight container with desiccant for maximum longevity.
