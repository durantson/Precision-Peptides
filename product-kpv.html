Immunomodulatory Mechanism

KPV exerts potent anti-inflammatory effects by interacting directly with the melanocortin receptors and inhibiting NF-κB signaling pathways. Due to its small molecular size, it exhibits superior permeability in cellular research models.

Format5mg / 10mg Lyophilized

Analytical & Stability Data

NF-κB Inhibition

Research models utilize KPV to study the suppression of pro-inflammatory cytokines (IL-1β, IL-6, and TNF-α). Unlike larger peptides, KPV remains stable across a wider pH range, facilitating gut-based research.

Stability Profile

Lyophilized KPV is highly resistant to enzymatic degradation. Experimental data indicates high structural integrity when stored at 4°C for short-term and -20°C for longitudinal studies.
