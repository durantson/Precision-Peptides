Frequently Asked Questions

Lyophilized (powder) peptides are highly stable at room temperature for transit. While we use insulated packaging, ice packs are typically reserved for liquid-state reagents or extreme climate zones to ensure 99.8% purity upon arrival.
Navigate to the "Verify Batch" link in our menu. Enter the batch number found on your vial's label to view the specific HPLC and Mass Spectrometry results for that sequence.
Lyophilized peptides remain stable for 24 months when stored at -20°C. Once reconstituted, reagents should be kept at 2°C – 8°C and utilized within 30 days for maximum research efficacy.
