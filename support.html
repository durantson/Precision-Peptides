Technical Knowledge Base

Verified laboratory protocols, reconstitution physics, and molecular stability data.

What is the "Wall-Drip" reconstitution method?
To prevent molecular denaturing, point the needle at the glass wall of the vial. Allow water to run down the glass rather than spraying directly onto the lyophilized powder.
Optimal storage for reconstituted liquid?
Store at 2°C – 8°C (refrigerated). Avoid the door due to temperature fluctuations. Use within 14-21 days.
How do I calculate concentration?
Concentration = Total mg / mL water. E.g., 10mg / 2mL = 5mg/mL.
Why is there a vacuum seal?
A strong vacuum pull ensures sterility and prevents oxidation.
