The Science of Purity

Amintex Labs was founded to solve the reproducibility crisis in academic research. We provide highly stable, HPLC-verified lyophilized reagents for institutional and private laboratories.

Synthesis Protocol

Our reagents are synthesized using Solid Phase Peptide Synthesis (SPPS). We utilize a strict purification process to remove trifluoroacetic acid (TFA) salts, ensuring a final acetate salt form that is safer for cellular cultures.

Verification

We operate on a "Trust, Verify, Verify Again" model. Every batch is subjected to High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) and Mass Spectrometry (MS) via third-party labs (Janoshik / MZ Biolabs) before being cataloged.

Cold Chain

Stability is paramount. Our inventory is stored at -20°C in climate-controlled facilities. Orders are packed with thermal insulation to prevent peptide degradation during the "last mile" of shipping.

Zero Tolerance Policy

99.0% Purity
Or It's Rejected.

The industry standard is often loose, accepting 95-98% purity which introduces unknown impurities into your research. Amintex Labs rejects any batch testing below 99.0%.
