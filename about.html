Our Mission

Eliminating Variable Outcomes in Research.

In the world of high-stakes biological research, the integrity of your reagent is the only variable that cannot be left to chance. Precision Peptides was founded to bridge the gap between "commercial grade" and "institutional grade" synthetic compounds.

We specialize in the synthesis of complex peptides and coenzymes, utilizing Solid Phase Peptide Synthesis (SPPS) and rigorous multi-stage purification to ensure that every vial meeting our catalog standards is ≥99% pure.

Purity Absolute

No vial leaves our facility without Mass Spectrometry and HPLC verification. Our purity benchmarks exceed industry averages by 15%.

Analytical Transparency

We provide full-spectrum raw data reports to our partners. We believe that researchers deserve to see the same data our chemists see.

Global Logistics

Operating from our central hub, we manage cold-chain distribution to research universities and private labs in over 45 countries.

Supply Chain Integrity

By controlling every step—from raw amino acid procurement to final vacuum-sealing—we eliminate the risk of contamination common in third-party brokered reagents.
