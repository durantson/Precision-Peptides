Optimized Permeability

NA-Selank Amidate is the most stable form of the Tuftsin-derived analog. By neutralizing the charges on both the N and C terminals, the molecule becomes more lipophilic, which is investigated for its potentially enhanced transport across the blood-brain barrier (BBB) and resistance to extracellular peptidases.
