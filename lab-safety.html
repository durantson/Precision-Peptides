Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

All research involving synthetic peptides must be conducted in a controlled laboratory environment. The following PPE is mandatory to prevent cross-contamination and accidental exposure:

  • 01

    Nitrile Gloves

    Double-gloving is recommended during the reconstitution of concentrated lyophilized powders.

  • 02

    Respiratory Protection

    A fume hood or N95 mask should be utilized when handling unsealed vials of lyophilized cake.

Safe Reconstitution Protocol

Ensure all solvent and vial surfaces are sanitized with 70% Isopropyl Alcohol prior to entry.

Pressure Equalization

Vials are vacuum-sealed. When introducing solvent, allow the vacuum to draw the liquid in slowly to prevent "spraying" or aerosolization of the reagent.

Hazardous Waste Disposal

Dispose of all utilized sharps and contaminated glassware in designated biohazard containers. Do not dispose of chemical reagents in standard waste streams.

Safety Data Sheets (SDS)

We provide comprehensive SDS for every molecule in our catalog. These sheets contain vital information regarding physical properties, toxicity, first-aid measures, and environmental impact.
