Mandatory PPE

Research involving synthetic peptides must be conducted in a controlled laboratory environment. The following PPE is required to maintain sample integrity and investigator safety:

01

Nitrile Gloves

Double-gloving is recommended during the reconstitution of concentrated lyophilized powders to prevent dermal absorption.

02

Respiratory Protection

A certified fume hood or high-efficiency particulate air (HEPA) mask must be utilized when handling unsealed vials.

Reconstitution Protocol

Aseptic Technique Mandatory

Pressure Equalization

Amintex Labs vials are vacuum-sealed. When introducing solvent, allow the vacuum to draw the liquid in slowly along the vial wall to prevent reagent aerosolization.

Laboratory Waste Management

Dispose of all utilized sharps and contaminated materials in designated biohazard containers. Do not dispose of reagents in municipal waste streams.
