Primary Research Data

Tirzepatide is a synthetic peptide engineered to function as a dual agonist for both the Glucose-dependent Insulinotropic Polypeptide (GIP) and Glucagon-like Peptide-1 (GLP-1) receptors. This dual mechanism is investigated for its synergistic effects on glucose homeostasis and lipolysis, offering a broader metabolic profile than single-agonist agents.
