Primary Research Data

Tirzepatide is a synthetic derivative of the gastric inhibitory polypeptide (GIP) which has been engineered to possess co-agonist activity at the GLP-1 receptor. Research models indicate that this dual-agonist approach creates a synergistic effect on glucose metabolism and lipid regulation. The peptide sequence contains a C20 fatty diacid moiety attached via a hydrophilic linker, which enables albumin binding and extends the elimination half-life.
