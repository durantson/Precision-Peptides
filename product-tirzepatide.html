Bio-Chemical Summary

Tirzepatide is a synthetic peptide consisting of 39 amino acids with a C20 fatty diacid moiety. It is investigated for its synergistic activation of both the Glucose-dependent Insulinotropic Polypeptide (GIP) and Glucagon-Like Peptide-1 (GLP-1) pathways.
