Logistics FAQ

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We ship to most countries via DHL Express. All parcels are labeled as "Chemical Reagents" with appropriate Harmonized System (HS) codes for customs clearance. However, the buyer is responsible for local import laws.

What if my item arrives warm?

Our peptides are lyophilized (freeze-dried), which makes them stable at room temperature for up to 8 weeks. The ice packs are a precaution to prevent extreme heat spikes. If the ice has melted, the product is still 100% effective.

Is the packaging discreet?

Absolutely. The shipping label identifies the sender as "PL Fulfillment". There are no references to peptides, research chemicals, or medical supplies on the exterior box.

My package is lost.

We offer "Route" shipping insurance at checkout. If purchased, we will reship any lost or seized package immediately. Without insurance, we review cases on an individual basis.
