Post-Arrival Protocol

To maintain the long-term stability of your reagents, it is vital to follow standardized laboratory storage protocols immediately upon delivery. Ambient temperatures are acceptable for short-term transit, but long-term preservation requires thermal stabilization.

  • Lyophilized (Dry): Store at -20°C for up to 24 months.
  • Reconstituted (Liquid): Store at 4°C for up to 21 days.
  • Avoid direct UV exposure and repeated freeze-thaw cycles.
