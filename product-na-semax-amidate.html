N-Acetyl Semax Amidate is an improved version of the original heptapeptide. The addition of the acetyl group and the amidation of the C-terminus provides significant protection against carboxypeptidase and aminopeptidase degradation, resulting in a significantly longer biological half-life in laboratory assays.
