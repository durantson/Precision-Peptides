Telomerase Activation Reagent

Epitalon is investigated for its ability to induce the expression of the telomerase reverse transcriptase (TERT) gene. This interaction is a primary focus for studies regarding telomere elongation and the cellular hayflick limit in senescent cell models.

Analytical & Longevity Research Context

Pineal Gland & Circadian Regulation

Epitalon was originally developed based on the bovine pineal peptide Epithalamin. Research suggests it may play a role in restoring melatonin secretion and normalizing circadian rhythms in aging biological models.

Genomic Methylation

Studies utilize Epitalon to observe changes in DNA methylation patterns. It is frequently explored for its potential to "reset" gene expression profiles to a more youthful state in vitro.
