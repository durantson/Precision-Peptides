Primary Research Data

AICAR acts as an analog of adenosine monophosphate (AMP) that is capable of stimulating AMP-activated protein kinase (AMPK) activity. In research models, AMPK activation by AICAR triggers metabolic changes such as glucose uptake and fatty acid oxidation, often simulating the cellular effects of exercise without muscle contraction.
