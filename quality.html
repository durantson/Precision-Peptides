Purity is our
Only Metric.

In peptide research, purity dictates reproducibility. Amintex Labs adheres to a strict "blind-testing" protocol. We do not release a batch until independent Mass Spectrometry confirms structural identity and HPLC confirms ≥99% purity.

The Manufacturing Chain

From amino acid coupling to final lyophilization.

01

Synthesis

We utilize Solid Phase Peptide Synthesis (SPPS). Amino acids are coupled sequentially onto a resin support. We use double-coupling cycles for difficult sequences to prevent deletion errors.

02

Purification

Raw peptides contain synthesis byproducts. We run Preparative HPLC to separate the target peptide. We aim to remove Trifluoroacetic Acid (TFA) salts, converting them to Acetate salts for better cell viability.

03

Lyophilization

The purified liquid peptide is frozen and placed under high vacuum. This sublimation process removes water without damaging the peptide bond, resulting in a stable, white sterile cake.

04

Validation

Before bottling, samples are sent to Janoshik or MZ Biolabs. We test for Purity (HPLC), Identity (Mass Spec), and Net Content. Batches under 99% are discarded.

Independent Verification

Trusting a manufacturer's internal data is a risk in research. That is why we utilize independent, third-party laboratories to blindly test our stock. We use the industry's most trusted names to ensure our clients receive exactly what the label claims.
