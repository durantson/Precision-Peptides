Purity is our
Only Metric.

In peptide research, purity dictates reproducibility. Precision Peptides adheres to a strict "blind-testing" protocol. We do not release a batch until independent Mass Spectrometry confirms structural identity and HPLC confirms ≥99% purity.

The Manufacturing Chain

From amino acid coupling to final lyophilization.

01

Synthesis

We utilize Solid Phase Peptide Synthesis (SPPS). Amino acids are coupled sequentially onto a resin support. We use double-coupling cycles for difficult sequences (like Retatrutide) to prevent deletion errors.

02

Purification

Raw peptides are full of synthesis byproducts. We run Preparative HPLC to separate the target peptide from impurities. We aim to remove Trifluoroacetic Acid (TFA) salts, converting them to Acetate salts for better cell culture viability.

03

Lyophilization

The purified liquid peptide is frozen and placed under high vacuum. This sublimation process removes water without damaging the peptide bond, resulting in a stable, white sterile cake / powder.

04

Validation

Before bottling, samples are sent to Janoshik Analytics or MZ Biolabs. We test for Purity (HPLC), Identity (Mass Spec), and Net Content. If it's under 99%, it is discarded.

Independent Verification

Trusting a manufacturer's internal data is a risk. That is why we pay thousands of dollars per month to independent, third-party laboratories to blindly test our stock. We use the industry's most trusted names.
