The Precision Standard

Every batch is subjected to a three-tier analytical verification process to ensure >99% purity.

Stage 01

HPLC Analysis

High-Performance Liquid Chromatography determines the chemical purity of the peptide by separating components based on molecular interaction.

Stage 02

Mass Spectrometry

Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (LC-MS) confirms the molecular weight of the peptide, ensuring the sequence is exact.

Stage 03

Stability Testing

Accelerated degradation studies are performed to verify the integrity of the lyophilized powder under various thermal conditions.

Interpreting the COA

A Certificate of Analysis (COA) is provided with every shipment. Our HPLC reports utilize a 214nm wavelength for maximum sensitivity to peptide bonds, revealing any potential impurities.

  • Purity Threshold: >99.0%
  • Moisture Content: < 5.0%
  • TFA Content: < 1.0%
