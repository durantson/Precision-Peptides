MCR Signaling Pathway

Melanotan II is a potent, non-selective agonist of the melanocortin receptors (MC1, MC3, MC4, and MC5). It is used to study the induction of melanogenesis (pigmentation) and the metabolic effects related to the central melanocortin system.
