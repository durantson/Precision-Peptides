Selectivity Profile

Ipamorelin is a selective GHS receptor agonist. Unlike other compounds in this class, research indicates it does not significantly elevate prolactin, cortisol, or ACTH levels in experimental models.

Stability Note: Reconstituted solution is most stable at pH 4.0 - 6.0. Avoid direct UV exposure.
