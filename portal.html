Standard operating procedures for reagent handling.

Cold Chain Integrity

Lyophilized peptides should be stored at -20°C for long-term stability. For active research phases, 2°C – 8°C is sufficient for up to 24 months in powder form.

Reconstitution Best Practices

Utilize Bacteriostatic Water (0.9% Benzyl Alcohol) for multi-use vials. Avoid mechanical agitation; use gentle swirling to maintain molecular structure.
